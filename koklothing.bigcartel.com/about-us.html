Our Story



In 2005, three friends founded a company called Ko Klothing to fund the next generation of business leaders. Starting with just a mere $200 and based out of a 750 square foot apartment, we began to resell all types of clothing. And now, twelve years later, we've grown to nearly 400 employees, have offices worldwide (Hey LA! Hello London! Hi Berlin!) and have funded over 3,000 future business leaders. Each of these leaders was sent to the State Leadership Conference host by the non-profit, Future Business Leaders of America, where each student is given the opportunity to immerse themselves in a business environment and network individuals from across the country. As time went on, we grew and grew — and were even named million users across platforms, Refinery29 has created a truly global community. In 2014, people around the world spent more than 3.6MM hours on the site, which is more than 150,000 days or 411 years. And, what were people reading in all that time? Everything. Stories ranging from runway trends and beauty DIYs to dating disaster stories and news and politics — all with our signature Refinery29 wink, of course. Our content is global, with features, contributors, and ideas spanning the world. And with it, we strive to make Refinery29 the number-one destination for stylish, intelligent women who crave thought-provoking conversation around limitless ideas.
